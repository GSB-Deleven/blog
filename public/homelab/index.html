Homelab Setup 2022

I have accumulated a whole lot of computers/tablets/phones/NAS/etc over the years. Thats why my HomeLab Build is probably a lot different the what you are used to.

Most of my machines have quite low specs, and several different Operating Systems, so I distribute the tasks between them. I handle most of it via SSH, it works ok like that.

Maybe one day i will have a powerfull enough machine to handle all of the tasks at the same time ;) I might also just throw proxmox on my Gaming PC one day and handle everything like that in one place. But for now it seems to work fine like it is.
